Privacy Policy for SeeAgain

We built SeeAgain to help you bring your photos to life. This page explains how we handle your information in clear, simple language.

Last updated: December 14, 2025

1. What we collect

When you use SeeAgain, we may collect:

  • Photos you upload so we can generate animations.
  • Animation prompts and settings so we can create the requested video and improve the experience.
  • Basic device and usage information (for example, browser type, approximate location, and how you interact with the site) to keep SeeAgain running smoothly.
  • Payment-related information (handled securely by Stripe) when you pay for additional animations.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate and deliver your photo animations.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve SeeAgain.
  • Prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and keep the service secure.
  • Process payments for paid features using third-party providers like Stripe.

3. How we handle your photos

Your uploaded photos are used to generate animations and to power features like prompt suggestions. We do not sell your photos or use them to train our own separate public models.

Third-party AI providers may process your images and prompts as part of generating the animation (for example, image-to-video models). We attempt to work with providers that offer strong privacy and security controls.

4. Payments

Payments for SeeAgain are processed by Stripe or similar third-party payment processors. We do not store your full credit card number on our servers.

Any payment information you provide is handled under the payment processor's own privacy policy.

5. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember basic settings, and understand how people use SeeAgain.

Where we use analytics tools, we aim to use them in a way that respects user privacy and avoids collecting more data than necessary.

6. Data retention

We keep your data only for as long as we reasonably need it to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

We may keep anonymized or aggregated data (that cannot reasonably be used to identify you) for analytics and service improvement.

7. Your choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights such as:

  • Requesting access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Requesting corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Requesting deletion of certain data, subject to legal and operational limits.

If you'd like to exercise any of these rights or ask a question about your data, you can email us at seeagainmoments@gmail.com.

8. Children's privacy

SeeAgain is not designed for children under 13. If you believe a child has used the service and provided personal information, please contact us and we will review and, where appropriate, delete the information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. In some cases, we may provide additional notice (for example, on the main site).

10. How to contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how SeeAgain handles your information, you can reach us at:

Email: seeagainmoments@gmail.com
